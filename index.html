About

Hello! I am currently a M.S. student at the Language & AGI Lab at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Jinyoung Yeo.

My research goal is to build intelligent AI systems that understand world knowledge to provide meaningful assistance to users. Mainly, I focus on two complementary directions: (1) developing AI agents that understand and act across diverse environments (digital and physical) to assist users through interaction, and (2) enhancing the knowledge and reasoning capabilities of language models to provide assistance through response generation.

Below are some keywords of my recent research interests:

  • (M)LLM Agents (Physical, Digital)
  • Physics & Spatial Understanding
  • RL for Long-horizon Tasks
