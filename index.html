About

Hello! I am currently a first-year M.S. student at the Data Intelligence (DLI) Lab at Yonsei University, advised by both prof. Jinyoung Yeo and prof. Dongha Lee. My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning, with a focus on enhancing models’ knowledge and reasoning capabilities.

I enjoy working on projects that involve both the theoretical and practical aspects of NLP systems, and I am always open to new opportunities, collaborations and open discussions :)

Below are some keywords of my recent research interests:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • (Mathematical, Logical, Clinical, ...) Reasoning
  • Conversational Agent
  • AI + X : Healthcare / Code
